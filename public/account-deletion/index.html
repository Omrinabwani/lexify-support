Account and data controls

Delete your account

Permanently delete your Lexify login and associated app data. Deletion cannot be undone.

Delete immediately in the app

  1. Open Lexify and sign in.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Under Account, choose Delete account.
  4. Review the data list, type DELETE, and confirm.

Delete securely from the web

Sign in below with the Lexify account you want to remove. Your credentials are sent directly from this browser to Lexify's Supabase Auth service over HTTPS and are never stored by this page.

Review the data list below, then type DELETE exactly.

Data removed

The authentication account and linked profile, learning progress, answers, practice history, collections, friend data, coins, and avatar data are removed from the live service. Limited backup or security-log copies may remain briefly for operational or legal requirements before expiry.

Need help?

Use the Lexify support form for technical help. It is public: never post your email, password, access token, or other private account data.

Read the Lexify privacy policy for more information.
