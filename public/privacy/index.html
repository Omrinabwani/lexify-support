Privacy policy

Lexify stores only the information needed to run your account and personalize your English learning.

Effective July 16, 2026

Data we collect

Account data: email address, display name, and authentication identifiers.

Learning data: native-language preference, goals, diagnosis results, vocabulary level, word progress, answers, practice sessions, streaks, and custom collections.

Social and customization data: friend code, relationships, nudges, coin balance, owned avatars, and equipped items.

Technical service data: Supabase may process IP address, user agent, request timestamps, and security diagnostics when operating the service.

How we use it

We use this data to authenticate you, personalize vocabulary practice, calculate progress and rewards, preserve learning history, operate friend features, prevent abuse, provide support, and keep the service secure. Lexify does not sell personal data or use it for third-party advertising.

Service providers and sharing

Supabase provides authentication, database, and backend infrastructure and processes data on our behalf. We disclose data only to operate Lexify, comply with law, protect users and the service, or complete a business transfer with appropriate safeguards. Lexify currently includes no advertising or behavioral-analytics SDK.

Storage, retention, and security

Your product data is retained while your account is active. We use access controls, row-level security, encrypted network transport, and authenticated server operations. No system is perfectly secure. Limited backup or security-log copies may remain for a short operational or legal period before expiry and are not available through the product.

Your choices and deletion

You can change preferences in Settings. You can permanently delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete account. Deletion removes the authentication account and associated Lexify profile, learning, practice, collection, social, and avatar data.

View deletion options

Children and policy changes

Lexify is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child supplied personal data, contact us so it can be removed. We may update this policy when the service or law changes; the effective date identifies the current version.

Contact

For general privacy questions, use the Lexify support form. The form is public, so never include account credentials or personal data. Account deletion is handled through the authenticated deletion page.
